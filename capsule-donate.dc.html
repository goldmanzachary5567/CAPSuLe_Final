Why We Need You
The translation gap

The research exists.
The translation is missing.

Developmental research that could change how parents raise kids and how teachers run classrooms sits in academic journals almost no one reads. CAPSuLe translates it and delivers it free. Films require crews, gear, and post-production. Courses require instructional design. None of it runs on goodwill.

Is my donation tax-deductible? +

Yes. CAPSuLe is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit. All contributions are tax-deductible to the full extent permitted by law. You'll receive a receipt for your records.

Can I designate my gift? +

Yes. You can designate your contribution to any active production fund (WordRx, Extended Release, MicroDose) or to the general operating fund.

Donation vs. sponsorship? +

Sponsorships are designated to a specific program and include named public recognition. General fund donations support overall operations with no credit required.

Do you accept recurring gifts? +

Yes. Recurring giving is especially helpful for sustained productions. Reach out to discuss a monthly or annual giving arrangement.
